About Webbed

Empowering users to take control of their online security with real-time client-side script analysis.

Our Mission

At Webbed, our mission is simple: To help every day internet users identify the possible vulnerabilities they may be exposed to online.

We believe that browsing the internet should be safe, and it’s our goal to make it possible for every user to easily identify and address security issues directly in their browser, without the need for complex setups or external services.

Our Story

We are a group of final-year students who came together to work on a project that would make a real impact on web security. With a shared passion for technology and a desire to solve real-world problems, we decided to focus on the vulnerabilities that exist in client-side scripts, an often overlooked aspect of web security.

As we researched, we realized just how many websites are vulnerable to attacks like XSS and script injections due to poor security practices in client-side code. That’s when we decided to build Webbed — a client-side script security inspector designed to detect these vulnerabilities in real-time, right within the browser.
